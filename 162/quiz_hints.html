Quiz 07 (Feb 10)

Physical Applications using Integrals (2)  - One question 10 points

The quiz will cover one of these questions. These types of problems will only contain metric units. Refer to my notes pages 1, 5-6 for relevant examples.

1) Work required to pump water out of a cylindrical tank

Recall the formulas 
F(x)=(density)(g)(Volume) 
Work on one slice of water = (Force) (Distance from slice to exit)
Total Work=integral of above work.

For this topic, we consider normal density = mass / volume.

2) Total force applied to the wall of a dam (due to water pressure)

Recall the formulas

Pressure = (density)(g)(depth)
Force on one slice of wall = (Pressure)(Area of slice)

Total Force = integral of above force
